About Us

  • We Code held its first hackathon in 2016 to create a safe environment for women and friends to share and learn technology together. Now in its fourth year, We Code hosts annual hackathons for participants of all levels and expertise to grow as engineers and shape the field of technology in Portland, Oregon.

    For more information, please contact info@wecodehackathon.com.

Meet the Organizers

  • Amber Milavec

    Creator and Chief Organizer

    In February 2015, Amber organized and ran her first hackathon, Hack Like a Girl, with the desire to create a unique space for women and friends to share and learn technology together. Later that year, she partnered with Puppet to create what is now known as the We Code Hackathon for women and friends.

    When not organizing hackathons, Amber works as a Principal Technical Architect in Nike Digital Engineering.

  • Lauren English

    Senior Operations Manager

    At Puppet, Lauren is a part of Operational Awesomeness for the Product Engineering Organization, in addition to being a three year WeCode Hackathon Alumni. Her spirit sister is Marie Kondo, an organizing consultant and star of the recent Netflix series Tidying up with Marie Kondo. Lauren loves animals, mountains, coding, humans, textiles, and food. Her forbidden love: all of the cheese.

  • Tiffany Longworth

    Non-Profit Wrangler

    Tiffany is the non-profit wrangler for WeCode and a Site Reliability Engineer at Zapproved. She has launched successful projects large and small, but has also worked on ones that were spectacular failures! In her work, Tiffany employs her background as a Marine, her training as an English teacher, the principles of automation, and cat gifs as much as possible.

  • Eric Putnam

    Volunteer Coordinator

    Eric has been a software engineer at Puppet for over two years and is a WeCode Hackathon Alumni. He is excited to be part of an event that aims to support the voices and skills of women and other marginalized groups in technology.

Meet the Judges
