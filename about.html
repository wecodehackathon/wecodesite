About us

Meet the Organizers

  • Amber Milavec

    Creator and Chief Organizer

    Amber organized and ran her first hackathon in February 2015 called Hack Like a Girl with the desire to create a unique space for women and friends to share and learn technology together. Later that same year, she partnered with Puppet to create what is now known as the We Code Hackathon for women and friends.

    Her day job is as Principal Technical Architect at Nike digital Engineering.

  • Lauren English

    Senior Operations Manager

    Operational Awesomeness for the Product Engineering Organization at Puppet. Three year WeCode Hackathon Alumni. Spirit sister: Marie Kondo. Loves: animals, mountains, coding, humans, textiles, food. Forbidden love: all of the cheese.

  • Tiffany Longworth

    Non-Profit Wrangler

    Tiffany Longworth is the non-profit wrangler for WeCode and a Site Reliability Engineer at Zapproved. She has launched successful projects large and small, but has also worked on projects that were spectacular failures! She likes using her background as a Marine, her training as an English teacher, automation, and cat gifs as much as possible.

  • Eric Putnam

    Volunteer Coordinator, Software Engineer at Puppet, WeCode Hackathon Alumni

    Eric has been an engineer at Puppet for 2+ years and is excited to be part of an event that aims to support the voices and skills of women and other marginalized groups in technology!
