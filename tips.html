Pro Tips

Things to keep in mind for your first hackathon

  • Supplies
  • Tech Prep
  • Team Work!
  • Self Care

1. Supplies

  • Check your bags before you leave. You'll need:
    • laptop
    • power cords
    • cables
    • dongles for presentations
    • thumb drive

2. Tech Prep

  • Use github to share code and keep version control with your team
  • Create a powerpoint or keynote template in advance
  • Set up your local environment: ide, docker, etc
  • When in doubt, start with code you know (kickstarter, template)

3. Team Work!

  • Create wireframes/sketches/whiteboard roughs as early as possible
  • Break work up into small tasks
  • Keep communications flowing with your teammates
  • Check in with your team frequently
  • If you need to leave, let your team and organizers know
  • If you are unable to come back the second day, let your team and organizers know

4. Self Care

  • Take breaks
  • Sleep
  • Eat
  • Ask for help when you need it
  • Be confident. You got this
  • Have fun!
